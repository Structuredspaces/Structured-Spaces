Deliver & Support

It starts with a simple enquiry. Tell me about your space, send a few photos, and within 24 hours you'll have a personalised quote in your inbox. No chasing. No waiting. If you're happy to move forward, a 50% deposit secures your spot and we book your consultation for whenever suits you best.

A relaxed 45 to 60 minute call where we dig into everything: your space, your guests, your goals. We'll talk about what's working, what's not, what colours you love, what budget you're working with, how your rates are sitting, and how you're feeling about the whole thing. No question is too small. By the end, I'll know your space almost as well as you do.

I take everything from our call and turn it into your personalised design guide, a clear, actionable document tailored to your space and your package. Room by room, detail by detail. What to change, what to keep, what to buy, and exactly where to put it. You'll receive it as a PDF, and if something doesn't feel right, you get a round of refinements included. This is your guide, it should feel like yours.

Once you're happy, the remaining 50% is settled and your final guide is yours to keep. But it doesn't end there. You can book a follow-up call where we go through everything together. I can help you position pieces when they arrive, check in on what you've implemented, or just answer questions as you bring it all to life. You're not left on your own.

About Me

I'm Tilly Thomson

I'm the founder of Structured Spaces. For years I've been rearranging rooms and styling spaces. It's instinct. I walk into a room and immediately see what needs to change.

Structured Spaces was born from a simple observation: potential is everywhere, but presentation is rare. I'd scroll through listings and see B&Bs with beautiful bones let down by small, fixable details. Furniture that fought the room. Spaces that felt cold when they should have felt inviting. Listings that didn't reflect the care clearly put into them.

I knew I could bridge that gap.

I work exclusively with B&Bs and boutique stays. My approach is research-led, detail-focused, and designed to deliver results you can measure in your bookings. This isn't guesswork or trends. It's intentional, strategic styling that positions your space where it belongs.

You've invested in your property. I make sure that investment is seen.
